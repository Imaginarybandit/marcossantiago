Projects

Yelpcamp

Yelpcamp is the final project in Colt Steele's class. It is a websites that simulates a campsite marketplace. In this webpage you can make an account to create and update campsites. You can also view campsites and see their locations on a map.

Dispensary Page

This is a personal project that imitates a webpage for a cannabis dispensary. In it you can see promotions for the store. You can view a menu of the different products. You can also make an account to add and customize products. It also has cart that you can add products to.
